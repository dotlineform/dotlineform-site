# Selection Audit (Final‑Only)

> Timestamp: 2025-08-29 20:17:19 BST

## What you get

- **PREPARE SELECTION**: builds permutations and computes the subset chosen by your sampling (Stride or Random) and First‑N — *no images rendered*.
- **On‑canvas table**: shows the first K selected permutations (index → order string).
- **EXPORT SELECTED CSV**: writes `selection_indices.csv` with columns:
  - `perm_index` — index into the full permutation list
  - `order` — e.g., 0-2-1-3
  - `sampling_mode`, `stride_n`, `random_keep_pct`, `random_seed`
  - `first_n_applied` — the cap used after sampling

## Why this helps

You can review, archive, or share the exact subset to be rendered — ensuring reproducibility and helping collaborators review choices without generating images.
