# Analytics Roadmap

Analytics is on a retirement path. Tag management moves to Studio, document sharing moves to integrated Docs Viewer, and tag-package sharing is retired without replacement.

| suggested order | feature | status | depends on | outcome |
| ---: | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | [Analytics App Retirement](/docs/?scope=studio&doc=d-20260718-155350-d95b37) | proposed | [Tag Management In Studio](/docs/?scope=studio&doc=d-20260718-155350-a2d49c); [Share Document Packages](/docs/?scope=studio&doc=d-20260718-155350-c84e62) | retire the Analytics frontend/server and generic Data Sharing subsystem after their real responsibilities have moved or been explicitly retired |

Paused Analytics concepts remain available for reference but are not a reason to retain the application. Source-faithful document-package work now belongs under Share Document Packages; future tag assistance belongs under Studio and remains human-mediated.

## Current Position

Document sharing has moved to Docs Viewer, tag-package sharing is retired, and the generic `data-sharing/` repository subsystem is gone. `analytics-app/` now hosts only the retained canonical tag data and tag-management routes. Retirement remains proposed until [Tag Management In Studio](/docs/?scope=studio&doc=d-20260718-155350-a2d49c) moves that final owned capability.
